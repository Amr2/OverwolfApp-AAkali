Spell Tracker

Spell Tracker is an easy to use MANUALLY summoner spell cooldown timer overlay.

Spell Tracker is designed to help players track the enemy's summoner spells

Our main fnction is the overlay but we also have a desktop feature where the user is able to generate an MMR graph with an estimated current MMR value for the player along with a "top MMR" and "minimum MMR" portion

1. upon you open the league of legends client, Spell Tracker will connect to the client and get your Summoner name to get the MMR graph data.

2. If the user didn't play enough Ranked games lately there will be not enough data for analysis.
and Unfortunately the Graph data will only Generated on 3 servers only (EUW,NA ,EUNE)
but if u are not in those regions the in game overlay will still work.

3. when you re-login the same operation will happen again to get the summoner data.

4. when there is enough ranked games played lately the graph data will be generated. and here you will get a Summery about your MMR.

5. and there the MMR Graph where Current MMR is the Expected MMR and Max MMR and Min MMR are mistake percentage Lines.

6. and u can control the visibility of any line by clicking on its Label of the line.

7. After clicking on the label the Graph lines will be updated.
